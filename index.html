Enzi Entrepreneurship.

Connecting Students to the Energy Revolution

Discover Enzi

At Enzi, we are revolutionizing the bodaboda (motorbike taxi) community with our cutting-edge e-mobility solutions. Our focus extends beyond just transportation; we're actively building a new energy economy that challenges the traditional gasoline industry. By designing electric motorcycles tailored for the Kenyan Taxi and delivery market, we're not just enhancing revenue opportunities for boda boda businessmen but also committing to environmental sustainability. Our electric motorbikes, powered by lithium batteries, harness renewable energy sources like solar power, abundant on the equator, ensuring a greener, cleaner future for all.

Nairobi

The modernation of Africa is happening now. By 2040, 1.4 Billion people on the African Continent will have moved into urban areas. 1.4 Billion will need a way of transportation. Narobi lies at the heart of the rise of the electric vehicle. With a population 5 million, the boda market in just the city is generates 4 billion dollars a year. As gas prices rise a shift is needed. Enzi represents the begining of a continental revolution.
