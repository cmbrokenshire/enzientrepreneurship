Discover Enzi

Here at Enzi we build e-mobility solutions for the bodaboda (motorbike taxi) community through innovation that responds to their need for higher revenue. We achieve this by cultivating deep and meaningful relationships, to inspire, empower and transform lives using environmentally friendly innovations that are mutually beneficial for all.

FEATURED LOCATION

Nairobi

The modernation of Africa is happening now. By 2040, 1.4 Billion people on the African Continent will have moved into urban areas. 1.4 Billion will need a way of transportation. Narobi lies at the heart of the rise of the electric vehicle. With a population 5 million, the boda market in just the city is generates 4 billion dollars a year. As gas prices rise a shift is needed. Enzi represents the begining of a continental revolution.
