Machine Learning Spring Research Associate - Infrastructure Optimization Internship - (8 weeks)

Enzi is an E-mobility company using the latest e-mobility technology to reinvent the operations of the Kenyan commercial motorcycle industry known as the boda industry. Our team makes electric motorcycles and battery swap stations which remove highly polluting motorcycles off the road and increase the take home pay of the drivers. Here at Enzi are revolutionising one of the largest economic drivers in Sub Saharan Africa, pushing technological boundaries to efficiently deliver a great product at a massive scale with aims of lifting a large segment of the population above the poverty line. The company is growing at an alarming rate, having raised over 1 million dollars, and now more than ever there is a drive to create a plan for the future. At Enzi we know, the better our blueprint, the more lives we can change.

It’s no secret that we want to change the world. In order to do that we first must onboard and develop the best people, starting with university students.

If you have a passion for solving challenging problems; learning how to build scalable, robust systems; love working with the latest technologies in a fast-paced, flexible environment; and are excited at the prospect of having a significant impact on software that could change millions of lives, then we want to talk to you.

Machine Learning Research at Enzi improves various aspects of our business but our interns will mainly focus on infrastructure planning and optimization. A core element of the business are the swap stations (where the batteries are swapped for new ones) and the charging stations (where the batteries are charged in bulk). As a Machine Learning Spring Research Associate interns will be using data gathered from our drivers to find the optimal location to place swap and charge stations within Nairobi.

In the spring of 2024, Enzi will be hosting a small number of unpaid Machine Learning Research internships. Applicants are encouraged to express their interest in one or more internships provided by Enzi.

We are looking for individuals with the following qualifications:

  • Experience programming in at least one programming language (e.g. Python, Scala, Java or C/C++).
  • Curious, self-motivated, and excited about solving open-ended challenges at Enzi.
  • Great communication skills, both oral and written.

Nice to have:

  • Publications in relevant conferences or journals.
  • Reviewable code repository demonstrating machine learning use in past projects.
  • Comfortable with Git
  • Comfortable with Tableu, PowerPoint, or other presentative devices
  • Comfortable with software engineering best practices (e.g. version control, testing, code review, etc.)
  • Experience working with Databases and cloud services
