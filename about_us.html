About Us.

Our Mission

Our mission at Enzi Entrepreneurship Program is give students the one of a kind experience of looking and taking part in the inner workings of succesful start. What makes a company successful? As an intern at Enzi you will witness how talent, hardwork, and determination are channeled into results. By taking part in this program you will be taking part in actual start-up experience, you will be part of a team that is struggling to make a difference in the world. Interns will be build software that could touch millions, present information that could change the trajectory of the company, and talk the clients interested in accelerating our growth. You can make a difference in the company that is already changing lives. We want to give you the opportunity to do good. The best opportunity we could possibly give.
