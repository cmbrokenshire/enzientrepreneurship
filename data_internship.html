Market Analyst- Data Strategist Internship- (8 weeks)

Enzi is an E-mobility company using the latest e-mobility technology to reinvent the operations of the Kenyan commercial motorcycle industry known as the boda industry. Our team makes electric motorcycles and battery swap stations which remove highly polluting motorcycles off the road and increase the take home pay of the drivers. Here at Enzi are revolutionising one of the largest economic drivers in Sub Saharan Africa, pushing technological boundaries to efficiently deliver a great product at a massive scale with aims of lifting a large segment of the population above the poverty line. The company is growing at an alarming rate, having raised over 1 million dollars, and now more than ever there is a drive to create a plan for the future. At Enzi we know, the better our blueprint, the more lives we can change.

It’s no secret that we want to change the world. In order to do that we first must onboard and develop the best people, starting with university students.

If you are enthusiastic about delving into complex economic challenges, eager to learn about market dynamics and technology trends, and enjoy applying the latest analytical methodologies in a dynamic and adaptable setting, then this opportunity is for you. We are looking for interns who are excited about the prospect of making a meaningful contribution to research that can influence the company's short and long term decisions . If this sounds like your passion, we would love to hear from you.

As Market Analysts at Enzi, our overarching goal is to enrich various aspects of our business operations. In particular, our Data Strategist Interns will engage in meticulous research to identify valuable data elements within our vast repositories. This involves not only discerning which data are most useful but also strategizing ways to enhance our data collection systems for optimal efficacy. A critical part of their role will be to investigate potential markets and identify prospective clients interested in purchasing this data. Through this comprehensive approach, our interns will play a pivotal role in transforming raw data into actionable insights and marketable assets.

In the spring of 2024, Enzi will be hosting a small number of unpaid Market Analyst internships. Applicants are encouraged to express their interest in one or more internships provided by Enzi.

We are looking for individuals with the following qualifications:

Must have:

  • Currently looking for those enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate degree program in Finance, Business Analytics, Economics, Math, Statistics, Accounting, or other majors with both substantial financial and statistics based coursework. (min GPA: 3.2)
  • Proficiency in at least one the following programing languages (e.g., R , Python, MatLab).
  • Proficiency with Databases and using SQL.
  • Skilled in Powerpoint, Word, and Excel.
  • Curious, self-motivated, and excited about solving open-ended challenges at Enzi.

Nice to have:

  • Previous experience doing financial research.
  • Good communication and presentation skills.
  • Strong problem-solving skills.

The Spring Internship:

At Enzi we offer a personalised experience for interns, and our aim is to offer an experience that mimics what it is like to actually work here. We match qualified interns with projects and groups based on interests and skill sets, and fully embed interns within those groups for the Spring. Each intern will be assigned a mentor that will work with them to develop their skills, advise them on their work, and accommodate their needs. Our Market Analyst interns will work directly with our program director to build and present market finding to the team! Enzi is a unique place to work and we live by our values, so it's worth learning more about us.

Internships are unpaid and are a minimum of 8 weeks. All spring internships will begin March 18th and will go until May 20th. Upon completion of the internship, students will receive a certificate of completion and a letter of recommendation based on their growth and contribution during their time at Enzi. Additionally, the internships are also eligible for University Credit.

Thank you for your interest in exploring career opportunities with our team. We are committed to attracting and nurturing exceptional talent and appreciate your consideration of our organisation for your professional journey.

As you prepare to submit your application, we would like to inform you of our comprehensive review process. Upon receiving your application, we will dedicate up to two weeks for a thorough evaluation of your credentials. If your qualifications align with our expectations and requirements, we will proceed to the interview stage of our selection process.

Candidates who advance to the interview phase will be required to participate in two 30-minute sessions. The first interview is designed to assess behavioural competencies, focusing on your soft skills, problem-solving capabilities, and alignment with our company's culture. The second interview will be technical, aiming to evaluate your specific skills and knowledge pertinent to the role.

Detailed information regarding the interview process will be communicated via email to those selected. We eagerly anticipate discovering the unique skills and perspectives you can bring to our team.
