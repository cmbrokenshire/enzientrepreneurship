Our Mission

At the Enzi Entrepreneurship Program, our mission is to offer students a unique, immersive experience in the dynamic world of successful startups. Ever wondered what makes a company successful? As an intern at Enzi, you'll get a front-row seat to observe how talent, hard work, and determination are transformed into tangible results.
