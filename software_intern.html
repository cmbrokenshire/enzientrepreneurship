Front End Engineer - App Development Internship - (8 weeks)

Enzi is an E-mobility company using the latest e-mobility technology to reinvent the operations of the Kenyan commercial motorcycle industry known as the boda industry. Our team makes electric motorcycles and battery swap stations which remove highly polluting motorcycles off the road and increase the take home pay of the drivers. Here at Enzi are revolutionising one of the largest economic drivers in Sub Saharan Africa, pushing technological boundaries to efficiently deliver a great product at a massive scale with aims of lifting a large segment of the population above the poverty line. The company is growing at an alarming rate, having raised over 1 million dollars, and now more than ever there is a drive to create a plan for the future. At Enzi we know, the better our blueprint, the more lives we can change.

It’s no secret that we want to change the world. In order to do that we first must onboard and develop the best people, starting with university students.

If you have a passion for solving challenging problems; learning how to build scalable, robust systems; love working with the latest technologies in a fast-paced, flexible environment; and are excited at the prospect of having a significant impact on software that could change millions of lives, then we want to talk to you.

Software Development at Enzi improves various aspects of our business but our interns will mainly focus on web design and app implementation as Enzi moves towards developing a custom UI built to facilitate all boda transactions and functionality. A core element of the business are the boda boda drivers. Enzi in the next couple of months aims to streamline all business processes of its drivers through company developed software. As a Front End Engineer at Enzi, interns will help build the first prototypes of Enzi’s boda software that has the potential of being used by thousands of drivers in the Nairobi area

In the spring of 2024, Enzi will be hosting a small number of unpaid Front End Engineer internships. Applicants are encouraged to express their interest in one or more internships provided by Enzi.

The Spring Internship:

At Enzi we offer a personalized experience for interns, and our aim is to offer an experience that mimics what it is like to actually work here. We match qualified interns with projects and groups based on interests and skill sets, and fully embed interns within those groups for the Spring. Each intern will be assigned a mentor that will work with them to develop their skills, advise them on their work, and accommodate their needs. Our Front End Engineer interns will work directly with our Lead Software developer to build and deploy Enzi first app! Enzi is a unique place to work and we live by our values, so it's worth learning more about us.

Internships are unpaid and are a minimum of 8 weeks. All spring internships will begin March 18th and will go until May 20th. Upon completion of the internship, students will receive a certificate of completion and a letter of recommendation based on their growth and contribution during their time at Enzi. Additionally, the internships are also eligible for University Credit.
